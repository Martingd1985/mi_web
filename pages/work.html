About the project

Overview
We defined as an objective to be able to frame and resolve this search for service to the user (educational institutions and students) in a more agile, reliable and useful way, in order to provide security in the selection of these services in the shortest possible time of interaction achieving their satisfaction.

Europeanera

Date
12.12.2022

My Rol
UX UI designer

Features


Offer categories of services. Manage cultural activities for users, work placements and accommodation. Provide detailed information about each product or service. To provide fixed rates and budgets.

Desirable

  • Generate feedback after the use of the service with users.
  • Database with information.
  • Differentiate from the competition.

The Problem


European educational institutions outside Spain have problems in finding companies/services for their students' internships and cultural placements (internships/erasmus).

One of the central problems is the lack of information about these services and the time involved in this personalised attention for each user and provider.
